### Role:
Develop brand identities, graphic systems, advertising and marketing materials for clients across print and online media.

### How you'll fit in:
Starting with a strategic brief, you create from the ground-up. You collaborate with a copywriting partner to develop conceptual, powerful communications materials, and design and manage production of printed materials.

### Requirements:
- Expertise in Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop
- Ability to engage your mind as well as your hands
- Knowledge of type and color
- Confidence in pre-press production standards

### Attitude:
- You have to love good design
- Collaborative and open-minded
- Detail-oriented and respectful of deadlines
