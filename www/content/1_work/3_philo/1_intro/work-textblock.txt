Subhead: Getting students to cut the cord but keep the content

----

Text: Philo came to us with a name, a killer product, and a question: How do we convince college kids to embrace an unknown service that changes their TV viewing habits?

With Philo, students have the power to watch live broadcasts and recorded TV on nearly any device. So we set out to prove to students that they could get the content they loved without being chained to a box in their room.
